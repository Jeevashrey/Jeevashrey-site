About Us

Established in 2022 by Miss Sakshi Dube with a vision to provide a haven for stray animals suffering on the streets. We provide 24/7 Emergency Rescue services to stray animals in need across Igatpuri, Ghoti & Kasara.

We also conduct sterilization (castration) drives and provide free Anti-Rabies Vaccinations. Our mission is to create a compassionate society that protects, loves, and heals all living beings.

Every year, we rescue hundreds of injured, abandoned, and sick animals, giving them a second chance at life. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure that no animal suffers alone.

Be a Part of This Mission

Jeevashrey (JAWS Igatpuri) runs purely on public support. Your donations help provide food, medical treatment, and shelter to hundreds of voiceless beings every year.
