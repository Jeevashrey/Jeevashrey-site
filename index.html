About Us

Established in 2002 by Miss Sakshi Dube with a vision to provide a haven for stray animals suffering on the streets. We provide 24/7 Emergency Rescue services to stray animals in need across Igatpuri, Ghoti & Kasara. We also conduct sterilization (castration) drives and provide free Anti-Rabies Vaccinations. Our mission is to create a compassionate society that protects, loves, and heals all living beings.
