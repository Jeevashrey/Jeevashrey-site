About Us

Established in 2002 by Miss Sakshi Dube with a vision to provide a haven for stray animals suffering on the streets, we offer 24/7 emergency rescue services across Igatpuri, Ghoti, and Kasara. We also run sterilization programs and provide free Anti-Rabies vaccinations for stray dogs.

Be a Part of This Mission

JAWS Igatpuri runs purely on public support. Your donations help provide food, treatment, and shelter to hundreds of voiceless beings every year.
