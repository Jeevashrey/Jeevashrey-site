About Us

Established in 2002 by Miss Sakshi Dube with a vision to provide a haven for stray animals suffering on the streets. Jeevashrey (JAWS Igatpuri) operates 24/7 rescue services for animals in distress across Igatpuri, Ghoti & Kasara. We also conduct sterilization drives and free anti-rabies vaccination programs. Our shelter provides lifelong care, food, and medical treatment to injured and abandoned animals. With the help of local authorities, NGOs, and kind-hearted donors, we continue our mission to heal and protect the voiceless.
